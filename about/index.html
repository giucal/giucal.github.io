A bout

This is Giuseppe, somewhere from Italy.

It’s /dʒuˈzɛppe/. And I/me are my pronouns. Jk. I haven’t pretensions to your familiarity with pedantry or The Beatles: this is not an “about you,” after all.

But then again, I don’t have any pretension, really.

I do write code sometimes. Most of it I don’t publish, and when I do it’s usually very simple, few-hours-of-work stuff. Ditto for text, which too I enjoy typing down.

I’m a chronic procrastinator and bit of a hair-splitter who, unsurprisingly, can seldom get anything meaningful done: or so they tell me. I think I agree, but like the cadence of “anal-retentive give-a-fucker” better. This to explain why there isn’t much to see here, I suppose. About the same, some nouns transposed, could be said to explain why there isn’t much in my life, either. But I digress.
